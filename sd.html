San Diego

San Diego is a major city in California and is just south of Las Angeles. San Diego's weather is always perfect, never too hot it's just perfect to go out to the beach and have a smoothie.

One of the attractions most people visit San Diego for is Legoland. Legoland is a very popular amusement park where everything is based on the building blocks that are legos.
